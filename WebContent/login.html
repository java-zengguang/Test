简介

  防灾科技学院隶属于中国地震局，是全国仅有的以防灾减灾高等教育为主、学科门类齐全的综合性全日制普通高等学校。学院始建于1975年。 学院面向全国招生，现有全日制在校生8000余人。

  学院坚持“人本治校、特色立校、人才强校、和谐兴校”的办学理念和“优化结构、深化改革、强化特色、提高质量”的人才培养思路， 立足防震减灾行业，面向经济社会发展，逐步形成了以全日制本科教育为主体，兼有硕士研究生教育、成人学历教育、 行业技术培训等并存的多层次、多类型、多形式的现代办学体系，培养具有强烈社会责任感、较强创新精神和实践能力的高级应用型人才。




Introduction

 The school is affiliated to China seismological bureau, and is the only comprehensive full-time institution of higher learning in the country with a comprehensive range of disciplines.The college was founded in 1975. The college is open to nationwide enrollment, and there are more than 8,000 full-time students.

 The college adheres to the concept of "people-oriented school, distinctive schools, strong talent schools, harmonious schools" and "optimizing structure, deepening reform, strengthening characteristics and improving quality". Based on the industry of earthquake prevention and disaster reduction, oriented towards economic and social development, it has gradually formed a full-time undergraduate education as the main body, with a master's degree education, adult education, education, Industry technical training and so on of multi-level, multi-type, multiple forms of modern school system, cultivate a strong sense of social responsibility, strong innovation spirit and practice ability of senior applied talents.
